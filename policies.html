The fine print, made friendly

Studio policies

A few simple things that keep every appointment running smoothly, so your set gets the full time and care it deserves.

Cancellations + reschedules +

A minimum of 24 to 48 hours notice is required for cancellations or reschedules. Every appointment is one on one, so a little notice means that time can be offered to someone else on the waitlist. Life happens though; if something comes up, just send a message as early as you can.

Running late +

Arriving on time means your set gets the full session it deserves. Running more than 10 to 15 minutes behind? Send a quick message; sometimes the design can be simplified to fit the remaining time, and if not, rebooking is easy.

Before your appointment +

Please come with clean, bare nails unless a removal has been booked. Extra time and charges may apply for removals, long lengths, nail art, or complex designs.

For Gel X toe sets, toenails need to be in workable condition and feet cleaned before the appointment so the tips can be fitted and applied properly. Not sure if yours qualify? Send a message on Instagram before booking.

For lashes, arrive with clean, makeup-free eyes. The experience page has the full prep rundown.

Removals +

Lash removals can be added to your appointment: $10 for sets applied at Novabeautytt and $15 for lashes applied by another lash tech. Mention it when booking so enough time is allowed for your service.

Nail removals are $10 and offered for Novabeautytt sets only. Removals for work done by other nail techs are not currently offered, so please keep that in mind when booking.

Refills +

Lash refills are priced at 2 week and 3 week rates, listed on the price list. Refills need enough of the original set remaining; past three weeks, a fresh full set is the way to go.

2 in 1 + duo appointments +

2 in 1: lashes and nails in one sitting, usually 3.5 to 4 hours depending on the style and service. Snacks and water are provided so you can relax and feel comfortable throughout.

Duo: book with a friend and get your chosen services at the same time, side by side. A great way to feel more at ease, knowing your friend is right there with you.

Looking for service questions instead? The FAQ covers lashes, nails, refills and removals. Anything else, send a DM to @novabeautytt before booking. Always happy to help.
